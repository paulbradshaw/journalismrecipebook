# How to write a reaction story

[Waitrose under pressure to reinstate worker sacked after stopping shoplifter](https://www.theguardian.com/business/2026/apr/06/waitrose-employee-sacked-stopping-shoplifter-walker-smith-easter-egg) follows a [previous story on the worker being sacked](Waitrose employee sacked after stopping shoplifter from taking Easter eggs)

Another example is [Sexual assault survivor calls failure of Travelodge boss to meet MPs ‘shocking’](https://www.theguardian.com/society/2026/mar/30/sexual-assault-survivor-calls-failure-travelodge-boss-meet-mps-shocking). This is a reaction to a story four days earlier - [Starmer tells Travelodge boss to engage with MPs over sexual assault case](https://www.theguardian.com/politics/2026/mar/26/starmer-tells-travelodge-boss-to-engage-with-mps-over-sexual-assault-case).
