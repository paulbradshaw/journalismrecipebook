# How to: write a news story based on 'concerns'

A common way to find a news story is to identify a trend and report on any concerns about that. An example of this approach can be found in the story *[‘Wild west’ reformer pilates boom is causing rise in injuries, experts warn](https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2026/apr/04/reformer-pilates-boom-injuries)*.

Here's how to do it:

1. Identify the trend
2. Make a list of potential interviewees
3. Make the approaches
4. Prepare for the interviews
5. Organise and conduct the interview
7. Write up the story
